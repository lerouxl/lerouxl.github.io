My Background / About Me
I'm a versatile AI scientist with expertise spanning both research and product development. Currently based in Toulouse, France, I work as a Data Scientist specializing in Generative AI at PredexIA, where I design and deploy hybrid RAG chatbots, fine-tune language models, and develop speech-to-speech applications.
My journey includes a PhD from Cardiff University focusing on Machine Learning Techniques for Metal Additive Manufacturing, where I developed Graph Neural Networks achieving 10x speedup versus finite elements and built CNN defect detectors with 96% precision. I bring strong technical skills in AI & Deep Learning, MLOps, and software engineering, combined with experience in academic and industrial collaboration across France and the UK.
I'm passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications, whether it's fine-tuning Mistral models on proprietary datasets, developing surrogate models for industrial simulations, or mentoring the next generation of AI practitioners.
