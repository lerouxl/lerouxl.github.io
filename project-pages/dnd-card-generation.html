D&D Card Image Generation
A creative exploration of generative AI applied to tabletop gaming, fine-tuning Stable Diffusion with LoRA techniques to create unique D&D character cards with custom artwork.
Project Overview
This personal project represents a fascinating intersection of my professional AI expertise and creative interests. I fine-tuned Stable Diffusion using Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) techniques to generate unique artwork for Dungeons & Dragons character cards, demonstrating the practical application of advanced generative AI in creative domains.
The project required careful dataset curation, advanced fine-tuning techniques, and creative prompt engineering to achieve consistent, high-quality results that captured the aesthetic and thematic elements essential to D&D character artwork.
